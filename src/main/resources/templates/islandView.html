Description: Island

Each day guests will have the option to choose two of the four activities. The days will be split into two time slots; Activity 1 in the morning and Activity 2 in the afternoon. We will be moving to a different island each day throughout the five-day trip.

List of Activity Options

  1. Scuba Diving

    Join our Scuba instructors on a 3 hour tour of some of the most unique, beautiful reefs in the world. The instructors will teach you about the natural wonders found beneath the water's surface! If you're lucky, you may even find Nemo!

  2. Spelunking

    This one's not for the faint of heart! Join our resident field geologist on a 3 hour caving adventure! You will learn about the natural cave formations found around the islands. By the end of the excursion, you'll be able to identify some of the types of rock formations. Wouldn't that be stalacTIGHT?!

  3. Hiking

    Go take a hike! I think I will! Join our two field biologists on a 3 hour guided tour of the island. You will find information about the flora and fauna as well as some of the ecology of the island. Make sure to bring comfortable, proper hiking footwear!

  4. Bird-Watching

    This is for the people that just want to soak in the views or recover from some of the other more physical activities. Join our ornithologist on a 3 hour guided light hike. You will be able to see and identify many species of birds found around the island. We provide the binoculars, but you may want to bring your camera.
